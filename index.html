Our Journey: Building Maintafox

Pioneering smarter maintenance management, designed for Algeria, supported locally.

Founded on data-driven principles, Maintafox delivers real-time insights and intelligent automation. We blend deep industry knowledge with advanced software engineering to create a powerful, intuitive platform backed by dedicated local support.

2023 - Birth of VibraFox

The idea sparked as VibraFox, focusing on vibration analysis for predictive maintenance in industrial rotating equipment.

2024 - Innovative Project Label

Recognized with the "Innovative Project" label by the Ministry of Knowledge Economy and Startups, validating its potential in Algeria.

2025 - Evolution into MaintaFox

The concept broadened into MaintaFox, a comprehensive, integrated CMMS tailored specifically for local industry demands.

June 1, 2025 - Official Launch

The first official version release, offering advanced, locally-supported maintenance management solutions to the Algerian market.

2026 - AI-Powered Predictions

Planning to integrate AI-driven predictive maintenance features, leveraging machine learning for optimized asset performance.

2028 - Expansion & Partnerships

Focusing on expanding reach within Algeria and exploring strategic partnerships to enhance the maintenance tech ecosystem.
